# Neogogy Readiness Index: 30 Questions

## Question Types Summary

- **Choice:** Multiple-button options with clear, distinct answers
- **Honest:** Open-ended reflection questions designed to elicit honest responses
- **Ranking:** Respondents rank multiple options by importance or value
- **Slider:** 0-100 scale for quantitative estimation

---

## Role Subjects

The prompts use `{{subject}}` placeholders that are replaced based on the selected role:

- **Teacher:** "In your classroom"
- **Parent:** "At your child's school"
- **Administrator:** "In your institution"
- **Student:** "As a learner"
- **Homeschool:** "In your home learning environment"

---

## Assessment Domains

All questions are mapped to one of four core domains:

1. **AI Role and Policy** — How AI is governed, named, and taught
2. **Teaching Practice** — How educators integrate AI into instruction
3. **Content and Curriculum** — How AI literacy and thinking skills are woven into the curriculum
4. **Assessment and Process Visibility** — How learning processes and thinking are made visible and assessed
